## About The Digital Literacy Project

The Digital Literacy Project is part of my Digital Literacy course. In The Digital Literacy Project I did a lot of work on tasks to learn how to use digital tools the right way and be responsible.

The main goal of The Digital Literacy Project was to learn how to make a presence communicate like a professional and stay safe on the internet.

---

## How The Digital Literacy Project Is Organized

The Digital Literacy Project is divided into folders for each task:

### task-1-presentation

This folder has my infographic about Digital Literacy. I made it using Canva to talk about things like safe internet use and useful tools for Digital Literacy.

---

### task-2-portfolio

This folder has pictures of my profiles on places like GitHub, LinkedIn and Kaggle. These websites help me build a presence online for The Digital Literacy Project.

---

### task-3-platforms

This folder has my work on coding and working together on platforms. It includes:

- A picture of a coding problem I solved on HackerRank

- A Google Form about being aware of Digital Literacy

- A picture of what people said when they filled out the form

---

### task-4-email-etiquette

This folder has:

- Two drafts of emails for The Digital Literacy Project

- A list of things to do and not do on social media

This task taught me how to talk to people properly online for The Digital Literacy Project.

---

### task-5-cybercrime

This folder has:

- A study of a case about phishing for The Digital Literacy Project

- A list of how to stay safe on the internet for The Digital Literacy Project

This task helped me learn more about cybercrime and being safe online for The Digital Literacy Project.

---

### report

This folder has my report on The Digital Literacy Project in PDF format.
