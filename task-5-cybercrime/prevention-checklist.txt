Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP or passwords with anyone  
- Always verify the sender before clicking any link  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication for extra security  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or payments  
- Check website URLs carefully before entering details  
- Keep your apps and system updated  
- Install antivirus software for protection  

For reporting cybercrime in India:
Website: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Helpline: 1930
